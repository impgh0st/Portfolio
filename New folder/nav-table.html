First, what is a full Stack Developer?

A fullstack developer is defined as a person that can work on and develop both the front-end and the back-end of a platform (With Databases, servers and clients) and are able to work independantly of other applications.

What does a full stack developer do?

The full stack developer uses a range of technologies and languages (see table below) to develop applications.

The Full Stack Developer job responsibilitys are:

Participating in the design and creation of scalable software, writing clean and functional code on the front- and back-end, and testing and debugging coding issues.
